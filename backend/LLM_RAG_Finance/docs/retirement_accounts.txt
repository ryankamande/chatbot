Retirement Accounts (US overview)

401(k): employer-sponsored; contributions may be pre-tax (traditional) or post-tax (Roth). Employer match is common; aim to capture full match if available.

IRA: individual retirement account; Traditional (tax-deferred) and Roth (post-tax). Contribution limits and income eligibility rules apply.

RMDs: Required Minimum Distributions for traditional accounts starting at a certain age; Roth IRAs typically have no lifetime RMDs for original owner.

General:
- Contribution limits change; check current IRS guidelines.
- Early withdrawals may incur taxes/penalties.
